About Monthaven

Monthaven Capital partners with principals, operators, and capital providers across essential real assets. We originate, underwrite, and package transactions, then either take a GP position (10–30%) or place opportunities with institutional/HNW partners. Our stance: off-market mandates, institutional diligence, and disciplined execution. We run a Notion-first, automation-heavy workflow for speed and consistency: T-12/rent roll parsing, comp libraries, sensitivity cases, and investor-ready OMs, with lender and operator relationships to assemble full stacks quickly and on-criteria.
