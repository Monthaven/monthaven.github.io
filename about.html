About Monthaven

Advisor, GP/Co-Sponsor, and LP delivering aligned capital with institutional process.

Monthaven Capital partners with principals, operators, and capital providers across essential real assets. We originate, underwrite, and package transactions, then either take a GP position (10 to 30%) or place opportunities with institutional/HNW partners. Our stance: off-market mandates, institutional diligence, and disciplined execution—grounded in the reasons clients choose us and the services we deliver.

What we do

  • Capital Advisory: buy-side, discreet dispositions, and capital formation.
  • GP / Co-Sponsor: structure debt/equity, manage diligence, and execute plans alongside operators.
  • LP / KP: selective co-investments with programmatic rights where useful.

We run a Notion-first, automation-heavy workflow for speed and consistency: T-12/rent roll parsing, comp libraries, sensitivity cases, and investor-ready OMs. Relationships with lenders, operating partners, and LPs let us assemble full stacks quickly, confidentially, and on-criteria. For a closer look at execution, review How We Work or request current briefs via the Intel desk.

This is not an offer to sell or a solicitation to buy any security. Any investment will be made only pursuant to definitive documents provided to qualified investors.
