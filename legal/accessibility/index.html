Accessibility commitment.

Monthaven Capital LLC is dedicated to providing a website and digital materials that are accessible to the widest possible audience.

Our approach

We design with WCAG 2.1 AA guidelines in mind, including sufficient color contrast, keyboard navigation, descriptive link text, and semantic markup.

Continuous improvement

We review this site regularly to identify and remediate accessibility issues. Third-party audits and assistive technology testing inform our roadmap.

Request assistance

If you encounter any accessibility barriers, please contact accessibility@monthavencapital.com. We will respond promptly to provide assistance or deliver materials in alternative formats.
